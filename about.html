THE CULINARY LOUNGE

Our Vision is to be one of the world's leading producers and providers of entertainment and education in the culinary space.

Going Beyond Food

The Culinary Lounge is here to give dining and cooking an all new meaning. Our unique experiential dining space will heighten the sense of taste, smell and sight on every plate.

At TCL, we believe food is the most primal and ideal way to bond with your family, friends or colleagues. There is no better stress buster than cooking up a delicious meal along with your loved ones and then sitting down to enjoy it together. We encourage children to explore the culinary world from a young age which helps them understand food sources and the processes involved in putting a meal together. It gives them a sense of ownership and inculcates in them a sense of healthy eating habits.

Whether you are looking to have a fun filled cook-off with your colleagues, a distinctive birthday party for your kids, an individualistic dining experience with your loved ones or wanting to learn specific culinary arts, our unparalleled global network of chefs have something for everyone, irrespective of age. They craft fresh, high-quality ingredients to bring you an experience that will stay with you long after you have left our premises.
